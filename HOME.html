Vishwakarma Mechfab is a trusted name in the field of heavy industrial fabrication and mechanical services. Established at our facility in the GIDC Area, Porbandar, we specialize in delivering top-quality services to industries requiring precision, expertise, and reliability.

Who We Are

Led by our Director, Mr. Bhavik K. Gajjar, Vishwakarma Mechfab boasts a team more than 60 skilled professionals who bring dedication and excellence to every project. With an annual turnover of ₹4.5 crores, we have grown into a preferred partner for clients across diverse industries.

We specialize in a range of industrial services, including:

- Heavy Fabrication and Erection
- Dismantling and Alignment
- Manpower Supply
- Services for Cement Industries & Rotary Kilns.

Our services cater to critical sectors such as:

- Cement Industry
- Chemical Industry
- Construction and Building Materials
